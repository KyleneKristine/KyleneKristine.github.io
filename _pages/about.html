About

Currently working at Brown University in the metadata unit, I use problem solving and programming skills to clean up and update the catalog. I recently graduated with a Masters in Library Science and have been working in the library field for over a decade now.

My interests lay with exploring the effects of Discovery platforms and metadata on relevancy; and improving workflows and gathering data using basic programming languages.

My attitude toward all my work is:

  • What can I learn from this?
  • How can I improve upon this?
  • What could I do better next time?
